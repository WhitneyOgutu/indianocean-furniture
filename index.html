For all your Furnishing needs

Indian Ocean Creations recycle and imortalise ancient dhows that have sailed their last voyage and we make furniture, carved doors and various other interiors of your choice.

Description

Historic, bespoke, handcrafted works of art made from ancient hardwood recycled from Dhows, Ngalawas and Canoes.

From the traditional sailing boats of the Indian Ocean to unique timeless pieces of furniture, each one individual in its form. Everything is handmade from reclaimed wood that has spent its life sailing the Indian Ocean. Indian Ocean Furniture only uses wood from old dhows and local ngalawas which are already disused and recyclable. These boats are built with different woods such as teak, mahogany and rosewood and sail at sea for an average of 40-80 years. Each piece is unique in color, style and size, each having its own story to tell. Designs can be chosen by you according to your taste, be it wax, natural oil or varnish. Our workshop in Nairobi can also create personalised furniture and completely custom designs. We have a showroom and workshop at the Nairobi Racecourse which offers both small objects such as sugar bowls, mirrors and lamps through to large pieces such as four poster beds, dining room tables, cupboards and Lamu doors cared by a traditional wood carver from the Coast.
